LinkedIn Cleaner

LinkedIn Cleaner is a Chrome extension that allows you to automatically delete pending invite requests on LinkedIn.

Getting started

- Go to the right page on https://www.linkedin.com/mynetwork/invitation-manager/sent/.
- Click on the button below to remove all requests from the page you are on.
WARNING: this action is irreversible! To stop, change page as soon as possible.
